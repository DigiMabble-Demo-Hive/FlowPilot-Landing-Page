The Challenge

Healthcare organizations face unprecedented email volume — placing patient outcomes and operational efficiency at risk.

Critical Communications Overlooked

Urgent patient symptoms and time-sensitive referral requests become buried within high-volume administrative correspondence, leading to preventable delays in care delivery.

Inefficient Resource Allocation

Clinical staff dedicate significant hours to manually sorting billing inquiries and scheduling requests — time that could be directed toward patient care and clinical priorities.

Priority Identification Gaps

Without intelligent prioritization systems, distinguishing urgent cases from routine correspondence becomes challenging — critical emergencies compete for attention alongside standard inquiries.

Regulatory & Compliance Exposure

Manual processing increases the risk of privacy oversights and SLA breaches — elevating legal liability and potentially impacting patient satisfaction and trust.

Priority Scoring & SLA Engine

Every classified email is assigned a severity level from P1 (Emergency) to P4 (Informational), with predefined handling protocols for each tier.

P1

Critical — Emergency

Immediate Bypass
Classification Logic

Life-threatening keywords or indications of immediate distress/harm.

Required Handling

Trigger urgent alerts (SMS/Page) to human staff. No automated replies generated.

P2

Urgent

Response within 4 hours
Classification Logic

Acute symptoms, post-procedure complications, or time-sensitive medication needs.

Required Handling

Route to clinical staff queue for expedited response within the defined SLA window.

P3

Standard

24 — 48 hour SLA
Classification Logic

Routine appointments, billing questions, and non-urgent record requests.

Required Handling

Automated processing via AI agent or standard queue within the defined SLA window.

P4

Low / Informational

Batch Processing
Classification Logic

General feedback, newsletters, out-of-office replies, and non-actionable notifications.

Required Handling

Archive, log, or route to low-priority administrative review queue.

How FlowPilot Works

A streamlined, fully automated pipeline from email ingestion to intelligent response.

1

Email Ingestion

Securely connects to your Microsoft Outlook account and fetches incoming unread emails in real-time.

2

AI Classification

AI engine analyzes content, identifies medical terminology, and classifies into the appropriate healthcare category.

3

Priority Scoring

Assigns severity from P1 (Critical) to P4 (Low) based on content urgency, medical context, and SLA requirements.

4

Intelligent Routing

P1/P2 alerts escalated to staff. P3/P4 handled via AI-generated contextual replies sent directly through Outlook.

5

Tag & Archive

Processed emails are tagged as "Replied" in Outlook, creating a complete audit trail for compliance and review.
